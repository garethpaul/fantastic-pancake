# Raw Batter Safety

status: completed

## Context

The guide covered cooked-pancake holding, leftovers, and allergens but did not
warn that flour and eggs are raw ingredients that can make uncooked batter
unsafe to taste.

## Work Completed

- Added a no-tasting rule for uncooked batter.
- Added child-safety and cross-contamination cleanup guidance.
- Linked current FDA and CDC primary guidance.
- Extended the content baseline to preserve the section and sources.
